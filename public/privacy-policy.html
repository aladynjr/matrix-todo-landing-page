Privacy Policy for Matrix Todo

Last Updated: March 2024

Introduction

Matrix Todo is a Chrome extension that replaces your new tab page with a Matrix-themed todo list. We take your privacy seriously and want to be transparent about our data practices.

Information Collection and Use

Matrix Todo operates entirely locally on your device and collects no personal information. Here's what you should know:

  • All todo items are stored locally in your browser's localStorage
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No personal information is collected or stored
  • No analytics or tracking systems are implemented

Data Storage

The extension stores only:

  • Your todo items
  • The completion status of tasks
  • Task categories (normal/urgent)

Permissions

The extension requires only the following permissions:

  • "chrome://newtab" - To replace the new tab page
  • "storage" - To save your tasks locally

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage, the security of your data depends on your browser's security. We do not handle any data transmission or external storage.

Changes to Tasks

You can:

  • Add new tasks
  • Mark tasks as complete
  • Delete individual tasks
  • Clear all completed tasks
  • All these changes are stored locally and can be cleared by clearing your browser data

Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us at:

  • Twitter: @aladdinnjr

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, there is no personal information to export or delete. You can remove all stored tasks by:

  • Using the clear completed tasks shortcut (CTRL + SHIFT + C)
  • Clearing your browser's local storage
  • Uninstalling the extension
